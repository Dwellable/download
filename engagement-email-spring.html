Spring in New England brings freeze-thaw, moisture, and everything waking up again. A little attention now really does save headaches later.

One thing to do this weekend

Take a slow walk around the outside. Look for cracks in steps or walkways, gutters that have come loose, or paint starting to peel. Five minutes can catch a lot.

A note on lawns

The grass will start to green up soon. Hold off on irrigation until around Mother's Day. Those late frosts can still do a number on pipes and heads.
